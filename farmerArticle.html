Local Farmers Innovate

A an example of farming innovation
Photo By Randy Fath
By Tractor Tractor
May 4, 2019
This weekend at a local agriculture convention a farming corporation displayed its new tachyon induced diametriec wheel grater. It is specifically designed to take in as much product as possible and return as little waste in the process as is humanly possible. the new model is based on an older impulse driven model that was underpowered and could only perform at half the speed of the newer model. The new model is also driven by an matter-anti matter containment field designed to move as much energy into the deflector grid as possible thereby inducing the plasma coils to burn off as much of their excess graviton energy as possible thus causing the tractor to consume far less dilithium then previous models ensuring maximum efficient operations as possible.
