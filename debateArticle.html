Presidential Debates are Heating up

A potential candidate delivers fiery prose
Photo By Eric Ward
By Bo Obama
May 4, 2019
  Here is an article completely designed to waste your time. It is going to give you an incredibly clickbaity title and dance around the issue for a long time before you realize by the end of this article you have wasted much of your time on nothing. I mean, the mystery of Banksy’s identity is one of the best kept secrets of the art world, compared to the likes of Robin Hood this mysterious artist seems to be very popular for some reason.

  Sometimes people feel anger after being tricked by a clickbait article, but other times it is resignation. After all you can never get the time back, time you could have spent doing literally anything else. What is the point even of all this, everything leads to a pointless mortal coil… the ice caps are melting the globe is burning and entire species of animals are being driven to extinction every day. And you, what are you doing? Reading this article? Seeking an answer to a question that doesn’t matter? Living your life as a bystander drifting to one moment to the next like a jellyfish.
