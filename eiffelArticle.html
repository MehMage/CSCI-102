France Weaponizes the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower on a perfect sunny day
Photo By Anthony Delanoix
By Agent P
May 4, 2019
  As France suffers yet another devastating loss of baguettes, the citizens of the fine country put their feet down. A project years in the making, President Emmanuel Macron unveils their deadliest addition to their arsenal, a weaponized Eiffel Tower. Modified to shoot a massive laser at any target on earth and in space, Macron cackled gleefully while twirling a newly acquired handlebar moustache. Macron then continued to go on a rambling and expositional speech detailing his childhood and past grievances that caused him to construct this world ending weapon. Re-christening the weapon to the Eiffel-tower-inator, the festivities were interrupted by a small greenish-aqua beaver like creature that appeared to be wearing a duck bill and fedora. In the ensuing bruhaha the tower self-destructed.

  Not to be outdone Queen Elizabeth II has claimed that the clock Big Ben is a device that can stop time, and that the Elizabeth tower can transform into a giant queen-mech that can shoot lasers out of its eyes and has rocket feet. When pressed for a demonstration, the Queen gave an evasive message of “it’s not feeling well at the moment”, then ran off yelling “tea time”.
