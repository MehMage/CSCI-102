New Art Exhibit in Downtown Bellingham

A new abstract structure in downtown Bellingham
Photo By Christian Fregnan
By Pseu Donym
May 4, 2019
  There is a new art exhibition in Bellingham. As local government officials struggle to figure out how it got there, it has already become a popular tourist destination. As people from all across Whatcom county flock to this strange piece of art, locals who live near the exhibit complain of loud noises at night and the occasional unearthly shriek that erupts regularly at 1:00 AM.

  According to an anonymous source that does not wish to be named, the artwork was placed there by the famous artist Banksy. The source then proceeded to cackle manically and run into the sculpture where he has not been seen since. Attempts to remove the sculpture have been largely unsuccessful as the nitroglycerin set aside to blast the sculpture was consumed by local Atlantean expert Milo Thatch. As local government officials debate on what to do about the sculpture, local stoners have set up camp on the southern side in hopes of seeing the rebirth of the eldritch god Cthulhu.
