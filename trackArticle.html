Sehome Track Meet Results

The runners vie to finish first
Photo By Braden Collum
By Marc Merrill
May 4, 2019
  In a tight race between the competing Bellingham Public Schools, this year will be one to remember as the track meet between these schools turned out to be fierce. There was not a boring moment as every race and event was neck in neck. Bellingham Highschool proclaimed victorious in the 100m and 200m sprints, however Sehome Highschool left them in the dust when it came to hurdles and relay. In the 5000m long distance Squalicum took gold edging out Options Highschool who took silver. In the Shotput event Sehome took another victory gaining first place. In the long jump category Options took the lead and won gold.
