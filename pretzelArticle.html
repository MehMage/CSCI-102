German Bakers Protest Pretzel Tax

A bakers holds up their pretzel as a symbol of protest
Photo By Pierre Gui
By Anne Wetzel
May 4, 2019
  The European Union in one fell swoop destroys the economy of Germany as they levy a 15% tax on all pretzels and pretzel related products. Furious, German bakers take to the streets to protest the tax as #MyPretzel becomes one of the top trending tags across social media. Proponents of the new tax claim that pretzels are too salty of a baked good and donâ€™t even taste that good. Lawmakers suggest that people choose other sensible alternatives like churros or baguettes.

  As chaos takes over Germany, a new black market for pretzels emerges with a self- proclaimed pretzel queen in charge what has now become the worlds third largest gang from her stronghold in Berlin. With all eastern Germany under her control, sources say that she has her eyes set north and will continue spreading her black-market pretzel empire into Sweden. International pressure mounts on the German government to control this rouge pretzel fanatic. Intervention by the UN peacekeeping force seems likely, as the government fails to suppress this elusive criminal mastermind.
